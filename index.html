HOW MIGHT WE MAKE YOUNG PEOPLE TO VISIT THEIR LOCAL MUSEUMS?

Reports show how people are not visiting museums unless they are tourists. They need to increase the number of visitors among mostly young people. In order to do that, my team and I carried an investigation, not only by a secondary research but also we performed 5 user interviews to people from 25 - 35 living in big and small cities and town to understand what are they leisure habits and what makes them choose an activity over another.
